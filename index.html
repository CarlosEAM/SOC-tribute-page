Props Road Fools

BMXing

Props Road Fools first season was released in 1998. The team behind the production took a bunch of riders, loaded them on to a trailer and drove around different states.

Each rider had their speciality, whether it was street or ramps. This would create an atmosphere where they would push the boudaries, trying out new and exiting tricks! while supporting one another.

The documentary style series would carry on for a total of 18 seasons. Every year the level and skill of the riders would improve. Always coming up with stuff you never thought would be possible. It is quite incrediable what people can do.

Of all the road fools seasons these is one which really stuck with me and that was Road Fools 14, check it out.
